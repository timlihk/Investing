## Company

Elite Material Co., Ltd. -- Taiwan's leading copper clad laminate (CCL) and prepreg manufacturer. 5th largest CCL maker globally. Acquired Arlon EMD (US-based high-performance laminate specialist serving aerospace/military). Products span halogen-free CCL, high-frequency/low-loss laminates, and IC substrates. Founded 1992, headquartered in Taoyuan, ~572 employees.

## SpaceX Relationship

**CONFIRMED** -- High-frequency PCB laminate materials supplied for Starlink terminal and satellite PCBs. Elite Material's product portfolio includes RF-grade, extreme-low-loss laminates critical for phased-array antenna boards and high-speed digital interconnects used in Starlink hardware. The Arlon EMD subsidiary specifically serves aerospace and military PCB applications.

## Why AVOID (at current price)

1. **Extreme valuation** -- TWD 2,910 at 71x trailing P/E. The stock has 7x'd from TWD 407 in one year. Market is pricing in years of 40%+ growth.
2. **SpaceX is a sideshow** -- the real driver is AI/HPC demand for high-layer CCL. Starlink is 1-3% of revenue at best.
3. **Competitors exist** -- Rogers Corp (ROG), Isola, Panasonic, TUC all make high-frequency laminates. Not a monopoly.
4. **Taiwan geopolitical risk** -- 100% Taiwan-based manufacturing for a critical material.
5. **Cyclical risk** -- CCL demand is tied to electronics capex cycles. Current growth may not be sustainable.

## Bull Case

- **Real business quality** -- Elite Material is a legitimate high-growth specialty materials company with improving margins.
- **Arlon EMD acquisition** gives aerospace/defense-grade HF laminate capability.
- **High-end CCL market** growing at 40% CAGR driven by AI infrastructure, 5G, and satellite communications.
- At a reasonable valuation (25-30x forward), this would be a strong HOLD. The company is good; the price is not.

## Verdict

**AVOID at current valuation.** Elite Material is a higher-quality SpaceX supplier than most Taiwan BOM names -- specialty HF laminates have real qualification barriers. But at 71x P/E and +379% in one year, the stock prices in far more than Starlink exposure alone. Would upgrade to HOLD below TWD 1,500 (35-40x forward P/E).

**One-liner:** Confirmed HF laminate supplier for Starlink PCBs; real moat but 71x P/E prices in perfection; buy the pullback not the hype.
