# Linde (LIN)

**Score: 30/100 | Chokepoint: 9/25 | Rating: HOLD**

## SpaceX Relationship: CONFIRMED

Confirmed SpaceX LOX (liquid oxygen) supplier. Linde built a ~$100M Air Separation Unit in Brownsville near Starbase and expanded its Mims, FL facility near KSC. Long-term supply agreements are in place.

However, LOX is a commodity product. Proximity to launch sites is the moat, not technology. SpaceX revenue is less than 0.3% of Linde's $34B total revenue — completely immaterial to the investment case.

## Thesis

Confirmed supplier with real infrastructure investment, but SpaceX exposure is a rounding error on Linde's financials. The proximity moat matters for supply reliability but does not move the stock. Not an investable SpaceX theme.
