# Renesas Electronics (6723.T)

**Score: 45/100 | Chokepoint: 16/25 | Verdict: HOLD**

> "The brains behind the beam — technically irreplaceable but financially immaterial."

## Company

Renesas Electronics — Japanese semiconductor company; dominant in automotive MCUs, expanding in SATCOM and IoT.

## SpaceX Relationship

Confirmed via teardown: Starlink user terminals contain Renesas GreenPAK SLG46620 (PoE controller) and F-series phased-array beamforming transceivers. Renesas holds the strongest SATCOM beamforming portfolio in commercial semiconductors. These are technically difficult to replace — but Starlink represents only 1-2% of total revenue.

## Financials

- **Revenue:** ~$9.65B
- **Market Cap:** ~$28-30B
- **Starlink est.:** 1-2% of revenue

## Why HOLD (not BUY on Starlink alone)

- Highest chokepoint score in this batch (16/25) — beamforming transceivers are genuinely hard to substitute.
- But Starlink is financially immaterial at 1-2% of a ~$9.65B revenue base.
- SpaceX volume growth helps but won't move the needle on Renesas earnings.

## Bull Case

- Buy for the automotive MCU cycle recovery + broader SATCOM beamforming trend across multiple satellite operators (OneWeb, Amazon Kuiper, Telesat).
- If SATCOM beamforming demand inflects across the industry, Renesas owns the critical silicon.
- Starlink validates the technology; the real upside is the category.
