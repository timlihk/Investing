# HEI - HEICO Corporation
## Date: 2026-03-28
## Score: 52/100
## Chokepoint Tier: Oligopoly with Moat (Broad Aerospace, Indirect Space)

### Thesis
HEICO is the world's dominant FAA-approved PMA aftermarket parts manufacturer and a leading niche acquirer of mission-critical electronic subsystems for defense, satellite, and aerospace. Its Electronic Technologies Group supplies components to satellite/spacecraft OEMs, providing indirect but unconfirmed exposure to SpaceX. HEI is a general aerospace/defense compounding machine, not a SpaceX-specific play.

### SpaceX Relationship: UNVERIFIED
No confirmed direct supplier relationship found in any public filing, press release, or credible source. HEICO's ETG subsidiaries (Lucix, Flight Microwave, VPT, Sierra Microwave, 3D Plus) serve satellite/spacecraft OEMs and defense primes who may in turn supply SpaceX programs, but direct commercial relationship is speculative. The PMA moat is primarily commercial aviation MRO, not rocket manufacturing.

### Value Chain Position
- **Flight Support Group (FSG)**: ~70% of revenue ($3.1B). FAA-approved PMA replacement parts at 30-50% discount to OEM. Airlines, MRO shops.
- **Electronic Technologies Group (ETG)**: ~30% of revenue ($1.3B). Mission-critical electronic, microwave, electro-optical components. ~51% of ETG to defense/satellite/spacecraft.
- Space-relevant subsidiaries: Lucix (satellite RF payloads), Flight Microwave (GPS III filters), VPT (power converters on GPS sats), 3D Plus (Mars Curiosity components).

### Moat Analysis
- **Strong (commercial aviation)**: 19,000+ FAA-approved parts, 500+ new PMAs/year. 30-50% cost advantage. Acquisition flywheel.
- **Weak (SpaceX/launch)**: PMA irrelevant to rocket manufacturing. SpaceX vertically integrated. ETG space components face competition.

### Catalysts
- Aerospace aftermarket growth (PMA penetration <50%), defense budget increases, 3-6 acquisitions/year, satellite constellation market growth

### Risks
- Premium valuation (~55-60x earnings), stock down 24% from ATH, OEM pushback on PMA, family-controlled succession risk

### Verdict: HOLD
Exceptional aerospace compounder but SpaceX thesis is weak — no confirmed relationship, PMA moat doesn't extend to rockets, space likely <5% of revenue. At $38-40B market cap and premium multiples, priced for commercial aviation story, not SpaceX optionality.
