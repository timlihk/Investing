# Sphere Corp (KOSDAQ: 347700)
## Date: 2026-03-28
## Score: 52/100
## Chokepoint Tier: Oligopoly with Moat

### Thesis
Sphere Corp is a small-cap Korean company that pivoted from digital healthcare (LifeSemantics) into a specialty alloy supplier for SpaceX. Confirmed 10-year, ~$1.05B supply framework agreement for nickel-based superalloys used in Raptor engines, nozzles, combustion chambers, and launch infrastructure. Arguably the single most direct publicly-listed SpaceX supplier globally. However, corporate pivot is extreme (from $2M/yr healthcare to $70M/yr alloy trading), profitability is unproven, and the $1.05B is an extrapolation from $55M confirmed 2026 volume.

### SpaceX Relationship: CONFIRMED
- **Contract**: ~$1.05B/10yr framework through Dec 2035 (+3yr extension option). Disclosed via Korea DART filing Aug 2025
- **Products**: Nickel-based superalloys for Raptor engines, nozzles, combustion chambers, launch pads, propellant systems
- **Confirmed 2026 volume**: $55.48M
- **Supplying since**: 2023 (via subsidiary Sphere Korea)
- **Tier status**: Claims Tier 1 vendor / one of 5 global Tier 1 suppliers
- **Upstream integration**: Acquired 10% stake in Nickel Industries' ENC project ($2.4B valuation) for nickel feedstock
- **Critical nuance**: $1.05B is extrapolated from annual volume, NOT a hard purchase commitment

### Red Flags
1. **Extreme corporate pivot**: Digital healthcare shell → SpaceX alloy supplier. Common Korean pattern (shell + unlisted opco merge)
2. **Profitability unproven**: Revenue surged but margins volatile (Q1 profit, Q3 loss)
3. **Single customer**: SpaceX is overwhelming majority of revenue
4. **$1.05B is extrapolated**: Only $55M confirmed for 2026. Framework agreement, not firm PO
5. **Valuation stretched**: 10x+ forward revenue, no earnings, stock already up 670%
6. **No ADR/OTC**: KOSDAQ only — requires Korean market access (IBKR, Saxo)
