# Win Semiconductors (3105.TW) — GaAs Foundry for Starlink RF
## Date: 2026-03-29
## Score: 58/100
## Chokepoint Tier: Duopoly Chokepoint (upstream)

### Thesis
Win Semi is the world's largest pure-play GaAs foundry with ~65-79% market share. Confirmed Starlink supply chain participant since August 2020, fabricating GaAs MMICs for satellite RF components. Upstream chokepoint — virtually all GaAs-based Starlink RF components pass through WIN or its smaller rival AWSC. SpaceX exposure est. 5-10% of revenue, growing. But stock at 96x TTM P/E / 87% above analyst consensus.

### SpaceX Relationship: CONFIRMED (since Aug 2020)
- DigiTimes reported WIN entered Starlink supply chain August 2020
- WIN + AWSC together = >90% of global GaAs wafer production
- Filtronic (confirmed SpaceX SSPA supplier) likely sources GaAs/GaN from WIN or European foundries (UMS/OMMIC)
- STM uses BiCMOS (silicon) for phased-array — WIN's role is in higher-power RF (SSPAs, LNAs, satellite-side amplifiers)

### SpaceX Exposure: ~5-10% of revenue, growing
- Satellite broadly est. 8-12% of revenue (within infrastructure segment)
- SpaceX/Starlink is largest LEO customer but not the only one
- Could reach 10-15% by 2027-28 as Starlink Gen2/3 scales + E-band/V-band SSPAs proliferate

### Verdict: HOLD — strong chokepoint, stretched valuation
Legitimate upstream chokepoint with confirmed supply chain position. But 5-10% exposure is modest, 96x P/E prices in significant growth already, and all 3 fabs are in Taiwan (geopolitical risk). Best viewed as compound semiconductor infrastructure play with satellite optionality, not a pure SpaceX bet.

**Entry zone:** TWD 200-250 (near analyst consensus) would offer much better risk/reward.
