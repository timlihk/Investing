# TDG - TransDigm Group
## Date: 2026-03-28
## Score: 43/100
## Chokepoint Tier: Oligopoly with Moat (Aviation Monopoly, Not Space)

### Thesis
TransDigm is the premier "conglomerate of monopolies" in aerospace — ~90% proprietary, ~80% sole-source revenue, 60% gross margins, 53% EBITDA margins. Champion Aerospace subsidiary has confirmed space igniter customers (Blue Origin, Aerojet-Rocketdyne, NASA) but SpaceX is NOT named. SpaceX's vertical integration philosophy is antithetical to TDG's sole-source lock-in model. Stellant acquisition ($960M) adds sole-US-source space TWTs for satellites. Buy TDG for aviation aftermarket supercycle, not rockets.

### SpaceX Relationship: SPECULATED / INDIRECT
- Champion Aerospace lists Blue Origin, Aerojet, NASA for space igniters — **SpaceX NOT named**
- SpaceX manufactures own igniters, turbopumps, valves in-house
- SpaceX's philosophy specifically aims to avoid sole-source suppliers with monopoly pricing
- Stellant TWTs serve satellite comms, not launch vehicles
