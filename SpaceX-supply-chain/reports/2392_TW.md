## Company

Chicony Power Technology Co., Ltd. -- Taiwan-based power supply manufacturer. Products include switching power supplies, adapters, LED lighting, EV charging, and embedded PSUs for servers/data centers. Founded 2008 (spun off from Chicony Electronics 2385.TW), headquartered in New Taipei City.

## SpaceX Relationship

**CONFIRMED (teardown)** -- Chicony manufactures the internal power supply unit for Starlink Gen2 routers. The specific model is the **UTP-211C**, a dual-output PSU delivering 50V 2.4A for the Dishy (antenna) and 12V 1.25A for the router. This uses 802.3bt PoE standard. Chicony branding is visible inside the router enclosure.

Additional Starlink PSU models attributed to Chicony include UTP-201A and UTP-201S for Gen1, and UTP-231L for the Starlink Mini.

## Why AVOID

1. **Commodity power supply** -- switching PSUs are manufactured by dozens of companies globally. Zero lock-in.
2. **SpaceX could swap vendors in one product cycle** -- no aerospace qualification needed for a router PSU.
3. **Declining business** -- revenue -7.3%, earnings -38% in FY2025. Structural headwinds in adapter market.
4. **No pricing power** -- PSU market is brutally competitive with thin margins.
5. **SpaceX exposure too small to matter** -- 1.5-3% of revenue does not change the investment thesis.

## Bull Case (weak)

- High dividend yield (7.8%) provides income floor.
- Server power demand could recover with AI data center buildout.
- Starlink Mini and Gen3 terminals could incrementally grow PSU volumes.
- But none of these are SpaceX-specific investment cases.

## Verdict

**AVOID.** Confirmed Starlink PSU supplier via teardown, but commodity power supplies have zero chokepoint value. The stock is cheap but declining. Not a SpaceX play.

**One-liner:** Confirmed Starlink router PSU maker but commodity power supply with zero lock-in; buy for the 7.8% yield if you must, not for SpaceX.
