## SpaceX Relationship: CONFIRMED

THE confirmed Starlink chip partner. STM has shipped 5B+ BiCMOS RF chips purpose-built for Starlink's phased-array antennas. This is a decade-long co-design partnership, not a catalog purchase — STM developed custom Panel Level Packaging for SpaceX. Production runs at 5M+ chips/day with volumes expected to double by 2027.

STM supplies both user terminals and 10,000+ satellites in the constellation. Starlink likely represents $500M-$1B+ of STM revenue and growing. This is the backbone of Starlink's phased-array revolution.
