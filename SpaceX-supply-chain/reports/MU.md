# MU -- Micron Technology

**Score: 22/100 | Chokepoint: 5/25 | AVOID**

## Thesis

Confirmed in Starlink terminal BOM (4GB eMMC flash, part MTFC4GACAJCN-1M) via teardown analysis. However, this is a commodity memory chip with perfect substitutes from Samsung and SK Hynix. Starlink is a rounding error on Micron's $58B+ revenue base. Micron is a fantastic AI/HBM play on its own merits, but it is not a SpaceX chokepoint.

## SpaceX Relationship

CONFIRMED (teardown) -- 4GB eMMC flash (MTFC4GACAJCN-1M) in Starlink user terminal. Standard catalog part, not co-designed. Samsung KLMAG1JETD-B041 or SK Hynix equivalents are drop-in replacements requiring minimal requalification.
