# LFUS — Littelfuse
## Date: 2026-03-29
## Score: 22/100
## Chokepoint Tier: Commodity / Indirect

### Thesis
Highly probable that Littelfuse P6SMB TVS (transient voltage suppressor) diodes are used for ESD/surge protection in Starlink user terminals. These are pure commodity protection components — sub-$0.10 parts with dozens of alternatives from Vishay, Bourns, Nexperia, STM, and others. Littelfuse is a well-run industrial electronics company, but Starlink exposure is financially irrelevant and strategically meaningless.

### SpaceX Relationship: HIGHLY PROBABLE (unconfirmed)
- **No confirmed teardown identification** of Littelfuse-specific parts in Starlink terminals
- P6SMB series TVS diodes are industry-standard protection components commonly found in satellite terminals, routers, and outdoor electronics
- Highly probable that Littelfuse or equivalent TVS diodes are on Starlink power/data lines for IEC 61000-4-2 ESD compliance
- **No IR mentions**, no earnings call references, no filing disclosures related to SpaceX
- Littelfuse acquired Basler (Dec 2025) for grid/utility/data center, not space
