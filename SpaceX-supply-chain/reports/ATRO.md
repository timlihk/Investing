# ATRO - Astronics Corporation
## Date: 2026-03-28
## Score: 28/100
## Chokepoint Tier: Commodity / Indirect

### Thesis
Astronics is a mid-cap aerospace electronics supplier focused on commercial aircraft power systems, lighting, and test equipment. While its Test Systems division serves NASA, JPL, and ULA with space electronics testing, the company is overwhelmingly a commercial aviation play (~74% of Aerospace segment revenue from commercial transport). There is no confirmed, material SpaceX supplier relationship, and space/launch revenue is a negligible fraction of the business.

### SpaceX Relationship: UNVERIFIED
No confirmed, publicly disclosed supplier relationship between Astronics and SpaceX. Astronics' space electronics testing page references NASA, JPL, and ULA as customers but does not name SpaceX. Some speculative "backdoor SpaceX" stock lists include ATRO, but no 10-K filing, earnings call transcript, or press release confirms SpaceX as a customer.

### Value Chain Position
- **Tier**: Tier 2-3 supplier of aerospace electronics (power, lighting, test)
- **What they make**: In-seat power (EmPower), electronic circuit breakers (CorePower), cabin/cockpit/exterior lighting, automated test equipment
- **Primary customers**: Boeing, Airbus, business jet OEMs, airlines (retrofit), defense primes, NASA/JPL/ULA (test systems)
- **Position in space**: Peripheral. Test Systems provides automated test equipment for space electronics — small, non-proprietary capability

### Moat Analysis
- **Strengths**: 500+ active patents, decades-long OEM relationships, EmPower is industry standard for in-seat power
- **Weaknesses for space thesis**: Test Systems is small, declining (~8% of rev). Space is a sub-segment, likely 1-3% of total. No proprietary space hardware.

### Catalysts
- Commercial aerospace recovery, margin expansion (OM doubled 2024→2025), record backlog $674.5M
- Not space-driven catalysts

### Risks
- Net debt $325M against $43M FCF (7.6x leverage)
- Boeing production dependency
- Test Systems (space-adjacent) is actually shrinking
- $225M convertible dilution risk

### Verdict: AVOID
No confirmed SpaceX relationship. Space <3% of revenue. Zero chokepoint. Stock near ATH pricing in commercial aero recovery, not space.
