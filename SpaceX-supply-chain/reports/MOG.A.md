# MOG.A - Moog Inc
## Date: 2026-03-28
## Score: 52/100
## Chokepoint Tier: Duopoly Chokepoint (Government Space, Not SpaceX Primary)

### Thesis
Moog is a tier-1 aerospace precision components manufacturer with deep space heritage (60+ years), confirmed presence on Falcon 9/Dragon missions, and a dominant position in thrust vector control actuators, cryogenic isolation valves, and space propulsion components. However, Marotta Controls (private) — not Moog — is SpaceX's confirmed primary valve partner (30,000+ CoRe valves, 14-year relationship, Gwynne Shotwell visited their facility). Moog's real moat is government/NASA programs (Artemis, SLS, ISS). Optimus/Tesla robot angle is completely unsubstantiated.

### SpaceX Relationship: PARTIALLY CONFIRMED / MOSTLY SPECULATED
- **Confirmed**: Moog components flew on Dragon CRS-11 trunk via Falcon 9. Moog Space Vehicles designed to launch on Falcon 9
- **NOT confirmed**: No direct ongoing Moog-SpaceX supplier agreement. No Starship/Raptor mention
- **Key finding**: Marotta Controls is SpaceX's confirmed primary valve supplier — not Moog
- **Optimus**: Zero evidence linking Moog to Tesla Optimus BOM

### Value Chain Position
Tier 1-2: cryogenic isolation & vent valves, TVC actuators & controllers, space avionics (C&DH, EPS), bi-propellant thruster valves, spacecraft mechanisms (solar array drives, antenna pointing), shock/vibration isolation

### Moat Analysis
- **Strong**: 60+ years flight heritage, human-rated space-qualified, ITAR protection, installed base on Artemis/SLS/ISS
- **Weak for SpaceX**: Marotta Controls holds primary valve relationship; SpaceX vertical integration hostile to external suppliers

### Catalysts
- Artemis II/III missions, NSSL Phase 3 contracts, defense budget growth, margin expansion, potential acquisition target at ~$10B

### Risks
- SpaceX vertical integration (Raptor redesign cut parts 30%), Marotta has primary valve relationship, tariff headwinds (80bps), $1.08B variable-rate debt, stock near ATH

### Verdict: HOLD
High-quality defense industrial with genuine space heritage. But SpaceX relationship is secondary (Marotta is primary), Optimus angle is unfounded, and space/SpaceX-addressable portion is low single-digits of revenue. Buy for Artemis/defense, not SpaceX.
