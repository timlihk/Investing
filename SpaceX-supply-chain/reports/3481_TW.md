# Innolux Corporation (3481.TW)

**Score: 20/100 | Chokepoint: 10/25 | Verdict: AVOID**

## Company

Innolux Corporation — major TFT-LCD display panel manufacturer (Taiwan).

## SpaceX Relationship

Confirmed TEMPORARY FOPLP (Fan-Out Panel-Level Packaging) chip packaging supplier for Starlink RF chips. However, SpaceX is building its own FOPLP plant in Texas (construction start Q3 2026, full production Q1 2027). Revenue from FOPLP work is ~NT$100M over 3 quarters, representing <0.05% of Innolux total revenue. This is a bridge supplier with an expiration date.

## Financials

- **Stock Price:** ~TWD 16.30
- **Market Cap:** ~TWD 118.6B (~$3.7B)

## Why AVOID

- SpaceX in-housing FOPLP packaging removes the entire revenue stream by 2027.
- Starlink contribution is financially negligible (<0.05% of revenue).
- No switching-cost moat; SpaceX chose Innolux for speed, not lock-in.
- Any investment thesis here is a display-cycle turnaround play, not a Starlink play.
