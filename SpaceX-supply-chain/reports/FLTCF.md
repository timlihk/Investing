### Contract Structure (Deep DD — Critical Detail)
- **Minimum $10M/year in orders** — SpaceX must place this as a floor, delivered within 12 months
- **SpaceX equity warrants**: 21.7M Tranche 1+2 warrants giving SpaceX up to **10% equity at 33p strike**. Tranche 1 (5%) vests at $37M cumulative orders. Tranche 2 (5%) vests on orders at **other frequency bands** (expansion beyond E-band)
- **Additional 10.9M warrants** at 92.8p strike (March 2025) — total potential SpaceX equity: **15%**
- Full vesting requires ~$60M cumulative orders. Warrants expire in 5 years if conditions not met
- **Interpretation**: SpaceX has economic incentive (cheap equity) to keep ordering. $10M/yr is floor, not ceiling

### Key Risk Mitigation

- Filtronic designs its own GaN MMICs in-house — not reliant on third-party chip design. Key differentiator vs. competitors
