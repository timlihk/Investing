## Why AVOID

1. **Commodity flash memory** -- 1Gbit SLC NAND is available from 10+ vendors globally. Zero switching cost, zero qualification barrier.
2. **Financially immaterial** -- <0.06% of Winbond revenue. SpaceX could swap to Micron, Samsung, or Kioxia tomorrow.
3. **No pricing power** -- NAND flash is a commodity with brutal price competition.
4. **Stock already ran** -- +375% in one year on memory cycle, now at 105x trailing P/E. Not a SpaceX play.
5. **SpaceX vertical integration irrelevant** -- SpaceX would never bother making flash memory, but they have zero dependency on Winbond specifically.

## Bull Case (weak)

- Memory cycle recovery could drive earnings higher, compressing the forward P/E to 5.7x.
- NOR flash leadership (world #1) is a real business moat -- but this has nothing to do with SpaceX.

## Verdict

**AVOID.** Confirmed but irrelevant -- a $1 commodity chip in a $12.8B market cap company. Buy Winbond for the memory cycle thesis, not Starlink.
