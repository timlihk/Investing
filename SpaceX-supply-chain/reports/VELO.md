# VELO - Velo3D Inc
## Date: 2026-03-28
## Score: 42/100
## Chokepoint Tier: Oligopoly with Moat

### Thesis
Velo3D is a turnaround story centered on a CONFIRMED, deepening SpaceX relationship. After near-bankruptcy in late 2024, rescued by Arrayed Additive (95% ownership). CEO confirmed 5 Raptor engine parts qualified exclusively on Velo3D platform, with active Raptor 4 production. $8M SpaceX licensing deal. Also pivoting to defense (Anduril, $32.6M FORGE contract). High-risk, high-reward bet on converting SpaceX foothold into sustainable profitability.

### SpaceX Relationship: CONFIRMED
- $8M licensing and support agreement ($5M license + $3M services)
- ~25 Sapphire PBF-LB machines installed at SpaceX facilities
- 5 major Raptor engine components qualified specifically for Velo3D platform
- Actively printing parts for Raptor 4 engine
- SpaceX was early investor, helped catalyze rescue restructuring
- **Critical nuance**: SpaceX owns and operates the machines. $8M license gives perpetual IP access. Risk of eventual in-house replacement.

### Value Chain Position
Mid-chain capital equipment and platform provider. Sells printers and software (picks and shovels model). Increasingly offers "Rapid Production Solutions" contract manufacturing.

### Moat Analysis
- **SupportFree printing**: Genuine technical differentiator for complex internal geometries (rocket cooling channels). Patented
- **Qualification lock-in**: 5 qualified Raptor parts = real switching costs
- **DoD STIG compliance**: First metal 3D printer to achieve Green-level
- **Narrow**: Nearly went bankrupt, larger competitors (EOS, Nikon SLM) have more resources

### Catalysts
- Raptor 4 production ramp scaling Starship/Super Heavy
- $32.6M FORGE defense contract + Anduril customer
- H2 2026 profitability inflection (>30% GM, positive EBITDA)
- SpaceX IPO narrative
- $22M Amaero exclusive supply deal

### Risks
- **Customer concentration**: SpaceX dominant customer
- **Profitability unproven**: Negative GM in FY2025, sustained cash burn
- **Dilution**: Arrayed Additive 95% ownership, $40-50M capex vs $39M cash — further raises likely
- **SpaceX in-housing**: Perpetual license means SpaceX has the IP
- **Competition**: EOS/Nikon SLM better capitalized. Nikon SLM MoU with Rocket Lab
- **Governance**: 95% owned by relatively obscure entity

### Verdict: HOLD (Speculative)
Genuinely interesting confirmed SpaceX play — the ONLY company in this universe with confirmed Raptor engine part qualification. But negative margins, near-bankruptcy history, dilution risk, and SpaceX in-housing threat cap upside. **If >30% GM in H2 2026 materializes, upgrade to BUY.**
