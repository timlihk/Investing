# Mynaric AG (M0YN.DE)

**Score: 5/100 | Rating: AVOID — BANKRUPT**

## SpaceX Relationship: NEVER EXISTED

Mynaric was NEVER a Starlink supplier. SpaceX builds its inter-satellite laser links entirely in-house. Mynaric's laser communication terminals targeted other constellation programs, not Starlink.

## Status

- Entered StaRUG restructuring (German insolvency)
- Delisted from NASDAQ in June 2025
- Shareholders wiped out
- Acquired by Rocket Lab for ~$75M (asset purchase, not equity rescue)

## Thesis

A cautionary tale for space supply chain investing. Mynaric was frequently pitched as a "Starlink supplier" despite having zero confirmed SpaceX relationship. The company failed to achieve production scale, burned through cash, and entered insolvency. The lesson: verify the actual customer relationship before investing. Proximity to the space industry is not the same as a confirmed SpaceX contract.
