# Trimble (TRMB)

**Score: 12/100 | Chokepoint: 4/25 | Rating: AVOID**

## SpaceX Relationship: NOT A SUPPLIER

Trimble is NOT a Starlink supplier. The company resells Starlink connectivity as an add-on for its agricultural display products — it is a downstream customer/integrator, not a supply chain participant.

More importantly, Starlink's emerging PNT (Positioning, Navigation, Timing) capabilities could actually DISRUPT Trimble's core GNSS correction services business over time. SpaceX is a competitive threat, not a revenue driver.
