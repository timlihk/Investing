## Bottom Line
STM's Starlink business is the highest-margin, fastest-growing segment at 23.4% OM and 23% YoY growth, buried inside a company trading at trough margins because of auto/industrial weakness. The market prices STM as an "automotive semi" — the Starlink premium is not yet reflected. SpaceX IPO could be the catalyst to re-rate. D2C satellites with 5x larger phased arrays are a chip demand multiplier. Starshield uses the same bus = same STM chips.
