# PH - Parker Hannifin Corporation
## Date: 2026-03-28
## Score: 30/100
## Chokepoint Tier: Oligopoly with Moat (Broad Industrial, Not Space)

### Thesis
Parker Hannifin is a diversified industrial conglomerate with a fast-growing Aerospace Systems segment (~31% of revenue, rising to ~35%). Makes cryogenic valves, fluid conveyance, seals, and quick-disconnect couplings for launch vehicles, including confirmed NASA SLS/Orion work. However, no confirmed direct SpaceX relationship. Space/launch is <1% of $19.9B revenue. PH is a high-quality industrial compounder, not a targeted SpaceX play.

### SpaceX Relationship: SPECULATED / UNCONFIRMED
- No public confirmation of direct SpaceX supply
- Documented space work centers on NASA SLS, Orion — traditional cost-plus programs
- Parker's Stratoflex (quick-disconnect couplings) and Bestobell (cryogenic valves) are technically relevant but no SpaceX contract evidenced
- SpaceX manufactures many components in-house (valves, turbopumps, avionics)

### Value Chain Position
Tier 1/2 component supplier: valves, seals, fluid conveyance, hydraulics, flight controls, thermal management, fuel systems. Stratoflex division for fluid conveyance, Bestobell for cryogenic valves.
