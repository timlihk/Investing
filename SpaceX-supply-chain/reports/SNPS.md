# SNPS -- Synopsys

**Score: 33/100 | Chokepoint: 11/25 | HOLD**

## Thesis

Confirmed EDA tool provider for SpaceX custom chip design (evidenced by SpaceX job listings requiring Synopsys tool proficiency -- VCS, Design Compiler, Fusion Compiler). SpaceX designs its own SoCs for Starlink satellites and terminals, and the EDA tool market is a duopoly between Synopsys (~60% share) and Cadence (~30%). Switching EDA vendors mid-design is extremely painful. However, SpaceX is one of thousands of chip design customers, and EDA tools are a fixed licensing cost -- SpaceX volume has zero impact on Synopsys revenue.

## SpaceX Relationship

CONFIRMED (job listings) -- SpaceX ASIC/chip design teams use Synopsys EDA tools for custom Starlink SoC development. This is a software licensing relationship, not a component supply. SpaceX likely also uses Cadence tools (most chip design teams use both). The Synopsys/Cadence EDA duopoly is a structural chokepoint for the entire semiconductor industry, not specific to SpaceX.
