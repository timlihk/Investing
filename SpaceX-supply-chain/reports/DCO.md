# DCO - Ducommun Incorporated
## Date: 2026-03-28
## Score: 42/100
## Chokepoint Tier: Competitive / Linked

### Thesis
Ducommun is a mid-cap aerospace Tier-2 supplier with record $825M revenue in 2025, strong missile/defense tailwinds, and legitimate space exposure through its Miltec subsidiary. However, the SpaceX relationship is unverified — no confirmed contract in any filing. SpaceX's 85%+ vertical integration makes it an unlikely high-value customer for a generalist structures/electronics supplier.

### SpaceX Relationship: UNVERIFIED
No 10-K, press release, or investor presentation names SpaceX as a customer. "Ducommun is a SpaceX supplier" claims come from generic investment listicles, not primary sources. Space work is primarily government-facing: AFRL plug-and-play spacecraft IDIQ (Miltec), NASA MSFC contracts, US Army SMDC work.

### Value Chain Position
- **Structural Systems (~43% rev)**: Contoured aluminum, titanium, Inconel aerostructures; hot forming; chemical milling; nacelle components; composites (VersaCore)
- **Electronic Systems (~57% rev)**: Circuit card assemblies, interconnects, cabinets, micro power supplies, missile guidance electronics, harness/cable assemblies
- **Miltec**: Missile and aerospace system design, spacecraft architecture R&D for AFRL
- **Key platforms**: Boeing 737/787, Airbus A320/A220, F-35, F/A-18, AMRAAM, SM-3, SM-6, Tomahawk, PAC-3, THAAD

### Moat Analysis
- **Moderate**: Qualification barriers (12-24 mo), platform stickiness (F-35, missiles have 20-40yr lives), VersaCore composites
- **Weak**: Not sole-source on most programs, no proprietary IP (unlike TransDigm), mediocre margins (10.5% OM vs TDG 40%+)

### Catalysts
- Missile production ramp (AMRAAM, SM-3, SM-6, Patriot, THAAD), record $1.1B backlog
- Boeing/Airbus recovery, Vision 2027 $950M-$1B target, engineered products mix shift (23% of rev)

### Risks
- $107.3M litigation settlement in 2025, SpaceX narrative overstated, 2.3x leverage, Boeing delays
- Valuation ~14x EBITDA, ~35x adj. EPS — not cheap for 10.5% OM business

### Verdict: AVOID (as SpaceX play)
Decent A&D supplier with real defense tailwinds but fails as SpaceX play: no confirmed revenue, SpaceX 85% vertical integration, low chokepoint (10/25), space primarily government-facing.
