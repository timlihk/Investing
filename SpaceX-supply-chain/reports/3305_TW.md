# Shenmao Technology (3305.TW)

**Score: 30/100 | Chokepoint: 9/25 | Verdict: HOLD**

> Note: Correct ticker is 3305.TW, not 3340.TWO.

## Company

Shenmao Technology — solder materials manufacturer (paste, wire, flux, BGA spheres) based in Taiwan.

## SpaceX Relationship

Confirmed solder materials supplier for Starlink PCB assembly. SpaceX has asked Shenmao to relocate production to Vietnam ($5M facility investment). Estimated Starlink revenue contribution: 5-10% of total revenue.

## Financials

- **Revenue:** TWD 8.21B (+34% YoY in 2024)
- **Starlink est.:** 5-10% of revenue

## Why HOLD (not BUY)

- Solder materials are a commodity with low switching costs.
- Direct competitors: Indium Corp, Senju Metal, Alpha Assembly Solutions.
- SpaceX could substitute suppliers without meaningful disruption.
- The Vietnam facility ask shows SpaceX leverage over Shenmao, not the reverse.
- Revenue growth is strong but driven by broader solder demand, not Starlink dependency.

## Bull Case

- If Starlink PCB volumes scale aggressively, Shenmao benefits from proximity and established qualification.
- Vietnam facility positions them for broader US-allied supply chain diversification trend.
