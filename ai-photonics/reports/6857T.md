# 6857.T - Advantest Corporation
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 71/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Advantest is the "ASML of semiconductor testing" -- the dominant supplier of ATE (automatic test equipment) for AI chips, with V93000 systems extended to support high-volume production testing of silicon photonics and co-packaged optics devices via a FormFactor partnership. Revenue raised to ¥1.07T with operating profit up 25-31% on AI-driven testing demand, capacity expanding to 5,000-7,500 systems to meet 30-40% growth. Every AI GPU, HBM chip, and now every photonic IC needs Advantest test equipment before shipping.

### Moat Analysis
**Rating: Very Strong (4.5/5)**

- **ATE market dominance**: Advantest and Teradyne form a duopoly in semiconductor ATE. Advantest dominates the high-end GPU/AI chip testing market.
- **V93000 SiPh extension**: V93000 platform now supports silicon photonics and CPO device testing -- every photonic IC needs testing before packaging.
- **FormFactor partnership**: Combined Advantest ATE + FormFactor probe cards creates end-to-end photonic IC test solution.
- **AI chip test monopoly**: NVIDIA, AMD, Broadcom all use Advantest systems. As AI chip volumes grow, test equipment demand grows proportionally.
- **Capacity expansion**: 5,000-7,500 system target signals years of growth ahead.

### Supply Chain Position
**Every chip needs testing**:
- **GPU/AI chips**: NVIDIA, AMD, Broadcom → tested on Advantest V93000
- **HBM memory**: SK Hynix, Samsung → tested on Advantest
- **SiPh photonic ICs**: Tower Semi, GF → tested on Advantest V93000 (extended)
- **CPO devices**: Tested before co-packaging with compute die
- **Partnerships**: FormFactor (probe cards), Tokyo Seimitsu (prober)

### Catalysts
1. **V93000 SiPh/CPO extension**: Direct entry into photonic IC testing market.
2. **Multiple guidance raises**: Revenue from ¥835B → ¥1.07T demonstrates accelerating demand.
3. **30-40% revenue growth FY26**: Capacity expansion to 5,000 systems supports multi-year growth.
4. **AI as primary growth driver**: Every new GPU cluster = more chips to test = more Advantest revenue.
5. **FormFactor photonic IC test partnership**: End-to-end solution for SiPh wafer to packaged die testing.

### Risks
1. **Japan-listed mega-cap**: ~$40-47B market cap limits re-rating potential.
2. **Cyclical semiconductor equipment**: ATE spending follows fab capex cycles.
3. **Teradyne competition**: Duopoly partner competes in certain segments.
4. **Valuation**: Already re-rated on AI demand. Premium pricing.
