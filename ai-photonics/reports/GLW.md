### Moat Analysis
**Rating: Strong (4/5)**

- **Fiber optic cable dominance**: Corning invented low-loss optical fiber and remains the global leader. >50% market share in North American fiber optic cable.
- **Meta $6B deal**: Multi-year supply agreement validates Corning as the strategic fiber partner for hyperscale AI buildouts. Lock-in at scale.
- **Innovation pipeline**: AI-optimized fiber and connectivity products (PRIZM TMT licensing, high-density fiber solutions) presented at OFC 2026.
- **Scale advantages**: World's largest optical fiber manufacturing capacity. Hard for competitors to match output quality at scale.
- **Diversification risk**: Non-optical segments (Display, Specialty Materials, Environmental) grow slower and dilute the AI narrative.

### Supply Chain Relevance
Corning is the **physical layer foundation** of all optical networking:
- Every Ciena (CIEN) WaveLogic system connects over Corning fiber
- Every Arista (ANET) switch's optical transceivers send light through Corning fiber
- Every data center campus built by Meta, Google, Microsoft uses Corning fiber infrastructure
- Upstream: raw glass, specialty chemicals
- Downstream: hyperscalers, telcos, enterprise networks

### Catalysts
1. **Meta $6B deal execution**: Validates multi-year demand visibility.
2. **Springboard plan**: +$4B annualized sales adds meaningful growth.
3. **Scale-out revenue $10.3B by 2030**: BofA sees 4x current AI fiber revenues.
4. **Data center enterprise +61%**: Fastest-growing sub-segment with strong momentum.
5. **New AI-optimized products**: OFC 2026 launches for high-density fiber connectivity.

### Risks
1. **Near ATH**: Stock up 91%, BofA target $144 is only 3% above current $140. Limited near-term upside at current levels.
2. **Non-optical segments**: Display tech, specialty glass are lower-growth and can drag overall results.
3. **Fiber is commodity-adjacent**: While Corning leads, fiber cable faces pricing pressure from Chinese competitors in non-hyperscale markets.
4. **Capex cycles**: Hyperscaler fiber spending can be lumpy. Meta deal provides visibility but others may delay.
5. **Margin profile**: 20% operating margin is good for materials but below pure-play semis (MACOM 35%, Credo 45%+).
