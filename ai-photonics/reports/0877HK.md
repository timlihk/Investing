# 0877.HK - O-Net Technologies Group
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 56/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
O-Net Technologies is a HK-listed Chinese optical component and module company that partnered with Sivers Semiconductors and Enablence Technologies on external light source (ELS) modules for AI data center co-packaged optics. The company manufactures optical amplifiers, isolators, couplers, tunable filters, and data center products -- essentially the passive optical component supply chain for Chinese transceiver makers. Less well-known than Innolight/Eoptolink but plays a critical component-level role.

### Supply Chain Position
- **Supplies to**: Innolight, Eoptolink, Accelink (passive optical components inside their modules)
- **Partners with**: Sivers (laser arrays), Enablence (star couplers) for CPO ELS modules
- **Products**: Optical amplifiers, pump lasers, ICRs, isolators, VOAs, WDMs

### Verdict: HOLD
O-Net is the passive optical component layer beneath China's transceiver makers. The Sivers/Enablence CPO partnership positions it for the emerging external light source market. HK listing provides better foreign access than Shenzhen-listed peers. Monitor for CPO/ELS revenue materialization in 2027+.
