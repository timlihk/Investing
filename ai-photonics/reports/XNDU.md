# XNDU - Xanadu Quantum Technologies
## Date: 2026-03-26
## Score: 40/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Xanadu is the FIRST publicly listed photonic quantum computing company, IPO'd on March 27, 2026 on NASDAQ and TSX with ~$302M raised plus CAD$390M in Canadian/Ontario government backing. The company builds photonic quantum computers using its PennyLane quantum software platform and Borealis quantum hardware. While quantum computing is still pre-commercial and not directly relevant to the AI optical interconnect thesis, Xanadu represents the bleeding edge of photonic technology — if photonic quantum computing works at scale, it validates the photonics platform for compute as well as interconnect.

### Verdict: AVOID (for AI photonics thesis)
Xanadu is a quantum computing play, not an AI optical interconnect play. The photonic technology overlap is conceptual (both use light) but the applications are completely different — Xanadu's quantum computer solves quantum problems, not data center connectivity. Interesting as a frontier technology investment but not part of the core AI photonics supply chain. Monitor if Xanadu pivots to photonic interconnect technology.
