# HUBN.SW - Huber+Suhner AG
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 58/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Huber+Suhner is a Swiss connectivity company that owns Polatis -- the industry-standard optical circuit switching (OCS) technology that Google, Meta, and other hyperscalers are deploying to dynamically reconfigure GPU cluster optical connections in AI data centers. The OCS market is projected to reach $2.5B by 2029 from nearly zero today, and Polatis/Huber+Suhner is the leading independent OCS supplier. However, at CHF 3.19B ($3.6B) market cap on CHF 1B revenue, OCS is still a small emerging segment within a diversified Swiss industrial company.

### Moat Analysis
**Rating: Moderate (3/5)**
- **Polatis OCS leadership**: Industry-standard OCS technology deployed at Google and expanding to other hyperscalers. First-mover advantage in a nascent $2.5B market.
- **Hollow core fiber research**: Advanced R&D in next-gen fiber technology.
- **Diversified company dilution**: OCS/data center is one of several growth initiatives alongside aerospace and rail communications.

### Catalysts
- OCS market reaching critical mass ($2.5B by 2029)
- Google expansion of OCS deployments
- Other hyperscalers adopting OCS (Meta, Microsoft)
- Record order intake (+13.7%) signals demand momentum

### Verdict: HOLD
Huber+Suhner owns the leading OCS technology in a market heading from near-zero to $2.5B by 2029. But OCS is still early, and the company is a diversified Swiss industrial with multiple non-AI segments. For investors with Swiss market access who want early OCS exposure, this is interesting. Lumentum (LITE, guiding $100M/quarter OCS by end 2026) may be a more liquid way to play the OCS theme.
