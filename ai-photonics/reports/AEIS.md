# AEIS - Advanced Energy Industries
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 65/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Advanced Energy provides precision power conversion systems for both the semiconductor equipment that manufactures photonic chips AND the AI data centers that deploy them -- a unique dual-exposure play. Data Center Computing revenue surged 107% in 2025 to record levels, with 25-30% growth guided for 2026. At $1.80B total revenue (+21%), $500M Q1 guide, and a Thailand factory adding $1B+ capacity, AEIS sits at the intersection of chip manufacturing power and AI infrastructure power.

### Moat: Moderate (3/5)
- Precision power conversion for plasma etch/deposition at 3nm and below -- critical for SiPh chip manufacturing.
- AI server power supplies consuming 5-10x traditional infrastructure -- direct exposure to GPU cluster buildout.
- BofA named AEIS as an under-the-radar AI pick.
- Competition from Delta Electronics, Vicor, Infineon in data center power.

### Supply Chain Position
**Dual exposure**:
- **Upstream**: Powers the semiconductor equipment (AMAT, LAM, ASML) that manufactures photonic ICs at Tower Semi and GlobalFoundries
- **Downstream**: Powers AI GPU servers and data center racks running the photonic transceivers

### Verdict: HOLD
AEIS offers a unique dual-angle on AI photonics -- powering both chip manufacturing and chip deployment. The 107% Data Center growth is impressive and the Thailand factory provides multi-year capacity. However, it's not a pure photonics play and competes with larger power companies. Interesting as a diversified AI infrastructure position.

**Final Score: 65/100**
