# PRY.MI - Prysmian SpA
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 60/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Prysmian is the world's largest cable company (€15B revenue) with a cutting-edge hollow-core fiber (HCF) partnership with Relativity Networks that could redefine AI data center interconnects -- HCF transmits data 50% faster and 1.5x farther than conventional fiber. While primarily a power cable company (not pure photonics), the HCF initiative positions Prysmian at the frontier of next-gen optical infrastructure for AI. A long-term option on hollow-core fiber adoption by hyperscalers.

### Moat: Moderate (3/5)
- World's largest cable company with global manufacturing scale.
- HCF partnership is cutting-edge -- if hollow-core fiber becomes standard for AI data centers, Prysmian has first-mover manufacturing.
- But HCF is still very early (partnership stage, not volume production).
- Fiber cable segment is smaller than power cables -- AI exposure is diluted.

### Supply Chain Position
- **Competes with**: Corning (GLW), Fujikura (5803.T), Hengtong (600487.SS) in fiber cable
- **Differentiator**: Hollow-core fiber technology (via Relativity Networks) -- potential next-gen replacement for conventional fiber in AI data centers
- **Downstream**: Hyperscalers, telcos, data center builders

### Verdict: HOLD
Prysmian is the European fiber cable heavyweight with a unique HCF angle, but it's primarily a power cable company where fiber/AI is a secondary driver. The Relativity Networks HCF partnership is genuinely interesting long-term (50% faster fiber could become standard for AI clusters), but volume production is years away. For near-term AI fiber exposure, Corning (GLW) is more direct. For long-term HCF optionality, Prysmian is worth monitoring.
