# 6869.HK / 601869.SH - YOFC (Yangtze Optical Fibre and Cable)
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 58/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
YOFC is China's largest and the world's #2 optical fiber and cable manufacturer (behind Corning), dual-listed on HKEX and Shanghai, with a cutting-edge hollow-core fiber (HollowBand) platform unveiled at MWC 2026 alongside an "AI x Fibre" technology vision. The company provides fiber optic preforms, fibers, and cables for AI data center infrastructure and is positioned as the Chinese national champion in optical fiber. Reasonable China fiber play with hollow-core fiber optionality, but carries the standard China access and geopolitical risks.
