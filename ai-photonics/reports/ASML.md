# ASML - ASML Holding NV (Context Note)
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 68/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
ASML is the monopoly supplier of lithography equipment (EUV and DUV) used to pattern every semiconductor chip including silicon photonics ICs. While SiPh typically uses DUV (not EUV) lithography, ASML's tools are essential for patterning the optical waveguides and modulators in every SiPh chip at Tower, GF, UMC, STM, and others. At $320B+ market cap and €28B revenue, SiPh is a tiny fraction but ASML benefits from every new SiPh fab (5 foundries now expanding capacity).
