### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
MaxLinear is a small-cap ($1.6B) optical DSP and retimer company competing with Marvell, Broadcom, and Credo in the 1.6T transceiver IC market. The Rushmore 1.6T DSP (sub-25W, Samsung CMOS) and Annapurna 224G retimer are sampling at OFC 2026. Revenue recovering from a trough (+30% to $468M in FY25) but still well below the $1B+ scale of competitors. An interesting turnaround play on the 1.6T ramp if Rushmore wins designs.
