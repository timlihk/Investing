# 042700.KS - Hanmi Semiconductor
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 70/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Hanmi Semiconductor holds 71% market share in TC (thermocompression) bonding equipment for HBM stacking -- the packaging bottleneck of the AI memory supply chain -- and has explicitly announced expansion into CPO (co-packaged optics) packaging equipment. Record revenue of KRW 576.7B in 2025 on HBM3E demand, with TC BONDER 4 launching for HBM4. However, SK Hynix is diversifying its bonder supply to ASMPT, potentially cutting Hanmi's share from 90%+ to 20-30% by 2026 -- a significant risk.
