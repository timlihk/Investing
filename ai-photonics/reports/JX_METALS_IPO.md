## Date: 2026-03-24

### Thesis
JX Advanced Metals is one of the world's dominant InP substrate manufacturers (alongside AXT), expanding capacity at its Isohara Plant (+20%) to meet surging AI optical transceiver demand. The company is scheduled for IPO in 2026, which would create the first publicly traded Japanese InP substrate pure-play. Parent company is ENEOS Holdings (5020.T).

### Why This Matters
- **InP substrate supply chain chokepoint**: Every EML laser, every InP photonic device starts with a JX or AXT substrate wafer
- **Japanese supply chain security**: Non-China InP source addresses geopolitical diversification needs (vs AXT's Beijing facility)
- **Capacity expansion**: 20%+ expansion at Isohara for hyperscale data center and AI edge computing demand
- **IPO timing**: If JX Advanced Metals IPOs during the AI photonics supercycle, demand for shares could be very strong

### Action: MONITOR for IPO filing date, expected market cap, and pricing
