# 002475.SZ - Luxshare Precision Industry
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 60/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Luxshare is a CNY 312B ($43B revenue) Chinese manufacturing conglomerate that has quietly built a full-stack AI data center capability spanning optical transceivers (10G-1.6T), connectors, thermal management, and cabinet-level integration. While primarily known as an Apple supply chain partner, Luxshare's data center division is now an OpenAI hardware provider with 400G/800G silicon photonics modules shipping and 1.6T in development. The optical transceiver business is growing but small relative to the $43B revenue base.

### Verdict: HOLD
Luxshare has impressive AI data center ambitions and a full optical transceiver portfolio, but the optical business is a small fraction of the $43B revenue base. Similar to Mitsubishi/Sumitomo -- great technology buried in a massive diversified manufacturer. For pure optical transceiver exposure, Innolight (300308.SZ) or Eoptolink (300502.SZ) are more targeted. Luxshare is better viewed as a diversified China tech manufacturing play with AI optionality.
