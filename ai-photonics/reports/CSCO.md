# CSCO - Cisco Systems
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 68/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Cisco is the sleeping giant of AI photonics -- with $5B+ in AI orders (FY26), ~$1.2B in AI optics revenue (40% of AI infrastructure mix), Acacia's ~50% coherent pluggable market share, and the new Silicon One G300 (102.4T switching ASIC), Cisco has quietly built a formidable optical networking and silicon photonics position. However, at $250B+ market cap, Cisco's AI/photonics business is diluted by the massive enterprise networking and security segments. Better as a diversified networking holding than a targeted photonics play.

### Verdict: HOLD
Cisco's AI optics business ($1.2B+) is meaningful but small relative to the $250B+ enterprise. Acacia's coherent pluggable dominance and Silicon One G300 are genuine competitive advantages. For pure AI optical exposure, Arista (ANET) or Ciena (CIEN) are more targeted. Cisco is the conservative, diversified play.
