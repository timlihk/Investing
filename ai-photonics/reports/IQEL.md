# IQE.L - IQE
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 52/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
IQE is a UK-listed compound semiconductor epitaxial wafer supplier making InP, GaAs, and GaN wafers used in optical transceivers, VCSELs, and RF devices for AI data centers. The company is emerging from a cyclical trough with strong H2 2025 demand driven by AI photonics and defense, and is under strategic review (attracting bidders). As a potential acquisition target by a larger photonics player, IQE offers event-driven upside, but the ~£97M revenue and breakeven profitability make it a speculative play on its own merits.

### Moat Analysis
**Rating: Moderate (2.5/5)**
- One of few global InP/GaAs epiwafer suppliers (alongside AXT, Sumitomo, JX Metals)
- Broad compound semiconductor portfolio (InP, GaAs, GaN, GaSb)
- Taiwan operations for VCSEL wafers (proximity to TSMC ecosystem)
- But small scale, barely profitable, and under strategic review suggests management doesn't see standalone viability

### Catalysts
1. **Strategic review / potential acquisition**: Bidders reportedly interested. Acquirer could be a larger photonics company (Coherent? Lumentum?) seeking epiwafer vertical integration.
2. **AI photonics demand recovery**: InP and VCSEL wafer demand growing from AI data center buildout.
3. **Defense spending**: GaN RF wafers for military applications provide a revenue floor.
4. **Cyclical recovery**: H2 2025 demand sharp improvement suggests 2026 could be a breakout year.

### Risks
1. **Barely profitable**: £2M EBITDA on £97M revenue. No margin of safety.
2. **Small scale**: Much smaller than AXT ($99M) and dwarfed by Sumitomo/JX Metals.
3. **AIM listing**: Low liquidity, limited institutional coverage.
4. **Strategic review uncertainty**: Could result in acquisition (upside) or failed process (downside).
5. **Cyclical business**: Epiwafer demand is tied to smartphone, telecom, and data center cycles.

### Verdict: HOLD
IQE is interesting primarily as an acquisition target -- a compound semiconductor epiwafer platform that could be strategically valuable to a larger photonics player seeking vertical integration into InP/GaAs substrates. On standalone merits, the barely-breakeven financials and small scale are uninvestable for most portfolios. Monitor for strategic review outcome. If acquired at a premium, the entry point is now; if review fails, the stock likely sells off.
