# 300394.SZ - Suzhou TFC Optical Communication
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 64/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
TFC Optical Communication is a major Chinese optical sub-assembly (OSA) and packaging company with $35B market cap and $662M TTM revenue, providing high-speed optical device packaging services to the AI data center transceiver supply chain. As the critical packaging layer between Chinese optical component makers (Accelink, O-Net) and transceiver module companies (Innolight, Eoptolink), TFC is the "Chinese Fabrinet" for optical sub-assemblies. Stock trading at CNY 325, this is one of the larger Chinese photonics pure-plays outside of Innolight.
