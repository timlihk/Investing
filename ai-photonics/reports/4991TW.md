# 4991.TW - GCS Holdings
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 57/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
GCS Holdings is a Taiwan-listed optoelectronic component company targeting mass production of 200G photodiodes and CW (continuous wave) laser products for AI data center optical transceivers in 2026. With 27% cumulative revenue growth and positioning across both receiver (PD) and transmitter (laser) components for silicon photonics architectures, GCS is one of the few pure-play Taiwanese optical component companies directly benefiting from the SiPh/CPO wave. Still small scale but growing into a critical component supplier role.
