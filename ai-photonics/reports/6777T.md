# 6777.T - Santec Holdings (Santec Corporation)
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 55/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Santec is a Japan-listed niche optical components maker specializing in tunable optical filters, wavelength selective switches (WSS), and tunable lasers used inside optical transceivers and networking systems. The company's MEMS-based tunable filters are used in 400G+ transceivers for noise removal, and it has launched ultra high-power tunable laser solutions specifically for AI-driven CPO (co-packaged optics) architectures. A micro-cap Japanese specialist addressing a critical but narrow niche in the optical transceiver assembly chain.
