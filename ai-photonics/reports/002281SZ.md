# 002281.SZ - Accelink Technologies
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 56/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Accelink is China's leading optical component manufacturer (optoelectronic chips, devices, modules, optical amplifiers, and data center transceivers), with revenue growing 36% to CNY 8.27B in FY2024. The company supplies the domestic Chinese data center and telecom markets with a broad product portfolio spanning from chips to subsystems. However, it lacks the NVIDIA/hyperscaler relationships of Innolight and Eoptolink, focusing more on the Chinese domestic market and telecom rather than AI GPU clusters.
