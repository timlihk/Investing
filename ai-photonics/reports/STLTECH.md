# STLTECH.NS - Sterlite Technologies Ltd (STL)
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 55/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Sterlite Technologies (STL) is India's leading optical fiber and cable company with $486M TTM revenue and a fast-growing optical interconnect business (+53% YoY with 30% margins). As AI data center buildouts expand globally, STL targets the fiber infrastructure demand from hyperscalers, with CRU projecting data center fiber demand growing at 76% CAGR from 2025. At $968M market cap, STL is the Indian play on AI data center fiber infrastructure.

### Moat: Moderate (2.5/5)
- India's leading optical fiber manufacturer with vertically integrated fiber preform to cable capability.
- Optical interconnect segment growing 53% at 30% margins -- higher-value data center products.
- Competes with Corning, Fujikura, Hengtong globally. Small in comparison.

### Verdict: HOLD
STL is the Indian entry point for AI data center fiber infrastructure investment. The 53% optical interconnect growth and 30% margins show the pivot to higher-value data center products is working. At ~$1B market cap, it's small but growing. For India-focused portfolios, this is the optical fiber play. For global exposure, Corning (GLW) offers more scale and hyperscaler relationships.
