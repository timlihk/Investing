# ONTO - Onto Innovation Inc.
## Date: 2026-04-12
## Score: 62/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Onto Innovation is a rising #2 player in semiconductor optical metrology and inspection, gaining share from KLA through superior software (Ai Diffract, SpectraProbe) in optical critical dimension (OCD) and advanced packaging inspection. The company's Dragonfly 2D/3D bump metrology platform is becoming critical infrastructure for AI packaging (CoWoS, HBM), with a $240M+ multi-year VPA and >30% advanced packaging growth guided for 2026. However, Onto is not a photonics-specific chokepoint -- it serves all semiconductor fabs, and KLA remains dominant with >50% metrology/inspection share; Onto is a well-positioned beneficiary of AI packaging demand rather than an irreplaceable bottleneck.

### Moat Analysis
**Rating: Moderate (3/5)**

Onto is gaining share but KLA remains the dominant force in semiconductor metrology/inspection:
- **#2 in optical metrology/inspection**: Onto's Ai Diffract and SpectraProbe software suites are winning OCD (optical critical dimension) tool-of-record slots at TSMC, Samsung, and Intel for GAA nanosheet processes. SemiAnalysis documented the share gain dynamic.
- **Advanced packaging strength**: The Dragonfly 2D/3D platform is becoming standard for bump metrology in CoWoS and HBM packaging. This is directly AI-linked -- every AI accelerator needs advanced packaging inspection.
- **JetStep lithography for advanced packaging**: Onto's lithography tools serve fan-out and redistribution layer patterning.
- **$240M VPA signals stickiness**: Multi-year volume purchase agreements indicate customer commitment, not just one-off tool sales.
- **NOT SiPh-specific**: Onto does not have dedicated silicon photonics metrology products. Its tools serve all semiconductor fabs. SiPh wafers could use Onto metrology, but this is generic semiconductor equipment, not a photonics chokepoint.
- **KLA dominance**: KLA holds >50% share in thin film metrology and inspection. Onto is the challenger, not the incumbent.

### Catalysts
1. **Advanced packaging >30% growth in 2026**: AI packaging demand (CoWoS, HBM, 2.5D) is the primary growth vector. Onto's tools are well-positioned.
2. **Record backlog (~2 quarters)**: Visibility is unusually strong for a semi equipment company.
3. **$240M+ Dragonfly VPA**: Locked-in revenue through 2027.
4. **3DI qualification at 2 HBM customers**: Volume orders could add meaningfully to 2026-2027 revenue.
5. **Q2 2026 guided above $300M**: Accelerating sequential growth trajectory.
6. **KLA share gain narrative**: If Onto continues winning OCD tool-of-record positions, the market re-rates the competitive dynamic.

### Risks
1. **Valuation at ATH**: Trading at 92.5x trailing P/E and 12.7x P/S near all-time highs. Any disappointment triggers sharp correction.
2. **2025 margin compression**: Gross margins fell to 54.6% from 52.2%, and net income dropped 32% YoY despite revenue growth. Mix or pricing headwinds need watching.
3. **KLA competitive response**: KLA has far more resources ($70B+ market cap vs Onto's $12.8B) and can invest aggressively to defend share.
4. **Cyclicality**: Semi equipment is deeply cyclical. When capex budgets tighten, metrology/inspection tools get deferred.
5. **Not a photonics chokepoint**: Onto does not specifically serve SiPh fabs with differentiated products. The connection to photonics is indirect (advanced packaging that may include photonic components).

### Valuation
At $257 and $12.8B market cap, ONTO trades at 12.7x 2025 revenue ($1.005B). If 2026 revenue reaches $1.2-1.3B (guided trajectory with Q1 at $280M, Q2 above $300M), forward P/S is ~10x. With 54% gross margins and recovering operating margins, this is a reasonable but full valuation for a semi equipment company gaining share. Fair value is $220-275 depending on 2026 margin recovery. The stock is near the high end of this range.

### Chokepoint Assessment: NOT A PHOTONICS CHOKEPOINT
Onto Innovation is a high-quality semiconductor equipment company benefiting from AI packaging demand, but it fails the photonics chokepoint test:
- It does not have SiPh-specific metrology products
- KLA is a larger, better-resourced competitor with >50% share
- Multiple alternatives exist (KLA, AMAT, Hitachi HT, ZEISS)
- The connection to photonics is indirect -- through generic advanced packaging inspection

### Verdict: HOLD
Onto Innovation is a well-run semi equipment company gaining market share from KLA in optical metrology, with strong AI packaging tailwinds. However, it is NOT a photonics-specific chokepoint -- it serves all semiconductor fabs generically. The stock is near all-time highs at a full valuation (92.5x trailing P/E). For photonics-focused investors, better options exist in companies that are actual bottlenecks (Tower Semi for SiPh foundry, Soitec for SOI wafers, FormFactor for SiPh test). Onto is a quality HOLD for semi equipment investors but does not belong in a photonics chokepoint portfolio.
