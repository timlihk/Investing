# AEHR - Aehr Test Systems
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 48/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Aehr Test Systems is the niche leader in wafer-level burn-in (WLBI) for silicon photonics, SiC, and GaN chips -- every SiPh wafer needs AEHR's equipment to stress-test defects before it becomes a transceiver chip. The company has volume production SiPh customers and received a follow-on order for AI optical I/O burn-in systems. However, at $880M market cap on ~$25-30M H2 revenue guidance, currently unprofitable with declining margins (29.8% gross), this is an early-stage equipment play with a long path to scale.

### Moat: Moderate (2.5/5)
