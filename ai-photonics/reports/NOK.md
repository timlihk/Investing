# NOK - Nokia Corporation
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 60/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Nokia is Ciena's primary competitor in optical networking with ~€3.4B combined optical revenue (post-Infinera acquisition), roughly 20% global market share. The Infinera deal brought InP DSP technology that Nokia combines with its own silicon photonics to create coherent solutions claiming 70% TCO reduction. However, Nokia is a €19.9B telecom infrastructure conglomerate where optical is one of several business lines, and the company is still integrating Infinera (€200M synergies target by 2027).

### Verdict: HOLD
Nokia is the Ciena competitor with similar optical market share but diluted by telecom infrastructure. The Infinera integration adds InP technology alongside Nokia SiPh. New coherent solutions at OFC 2026 are competitive. For pure optical networking exposure, Ciena (CIEN) is more targeted. Nokia offers diversified telecom infrastructure with optical upside.
