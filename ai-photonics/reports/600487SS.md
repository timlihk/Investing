### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Hengtong is China's #2 fiber optic cable maker (8% global share) with a unique dual angle on AI photonics: traditional fiber cable infrastructure PLUS advanced packaging capabilities for optical modules and CPO. Revenue growing 20-27% YoY to ~CNY 64B ($8.8B) annualized, with net income of ~CNY 3.3B and plans for an AI advanced optical fiber R&D and manufacturing center. Interesting as a China-listed fiber + optical packaging play, but carries the standard China access and geopolitical risks.

### Verdict: HOLD
Hengtong offers a unique combination of fiber cable + optical module packaging + liquid cooling for AI data centers, making it more than just a "Chinese Corning." The advanced packaging and CPO angle is genuinely differentiated. However, limited foreign access, China risks, and lower margins vs Corning make it a secondary choice. For investors with Shanghai Stock Connect access and China conviction, it's a reasonable fiber + packaging play.
