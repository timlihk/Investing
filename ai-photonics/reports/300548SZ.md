### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Broadex Technologies is a Chinese optical component company ($2.68B market cap, $274M TTM revenue) that designs and manufactures passive and active optical components including silicon photonics, PLC splitters, transceiver packaging, and high-speed cables for data centers and DWDM networks. Positioned as a mid-tier Chinese optical component supplier below Innolight/Eoptolink/Accelink in the market hierarchy, but with silicon photonics capability that could benefit from the SiPh adoption wave.
