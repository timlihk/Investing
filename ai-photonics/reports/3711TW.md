# 3711.TW - ASE Technology Holding (ASE Group)
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 70/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
ASE is the world's largest OSAT (outsourced semiconductor assembly and test) company with a dedicated silicon photonics packaging platform, CPO demonstrations achieving <5pJ/bit, and a strategic Lightmatter partnership for 3D-stacked photonics engines. As the packaging bottleneck for every AI chip (GPU, HBM, now photonic ICs), ASE's investments in SiPh packaging, optical engine co-packaging, and wafer-level photonic assembly position it as the infrastructure layer for co-packaged optics. FY2026 revenue expected to grow $1B+ with SiPh packaging starting full-process production in H2 2026.

### Moat Analysis
**Rating: Strong (4/5)**

- **World's #1 OSAT**: ASE packages more semiconductor chips than any other outsourced company. Scale advantage is massive.
- **SiPh packaging platform**: Dedicated silicon photonics packaging with CPO demonstrations. Covers optical engines from 400G to 1.6T.
- **Lightmatter partnership**: 3D-stacked photonics engine co-packaging -- the cutting edge of photonic integration.
- **WIN Semi plant acquisition**: Bought WIN Semi's Kaohsiung plant specifically for advanced AI packaging capacity expansion.
- **Packaging bottleneck position**: Every chip needs packaging. As photonic ICs scale, they all need ASE-class packaging.

### Supply Chain Position
**Packaging layer for ALL photonic chips**:
- **Packages chips from**: Tower Semi (SiPh), GlobalFoundries (SiPh), TSMC (logic+optics)
- **Partners with**: Lightmatter (3D photonics), Broadcom (CPO), NVIDIA
- **Competes with**: Amkor (AMKR), JCET, SPIL (subsidiary) in general OSAT; ASMPT (0522.HK) and Besi (BESI.AS) in equipment
- **USI subsidiary**: Merged with EugenLight to expand SiPh module capacity in Vietnam

### Catalysts
1. **SiPh packaging production H2 2026**: First full-process SiPh packaging, $300-400M revenue potential.
2. **Lightmatter 3D photonics**: Strategic partnership for next-gen photonic integration.
3. **CPO demonstrations**: <5pJ/bit achievement validates technology readiness.
4. **+$1B capex**: Aggressive capacity expansion for AI packaging demand.
5. **Revenue +$1B in FY2026**: CFO-stated growth target.

### Risks
1. **Large diversified OSAT**: SiPh is a small new segment within a massive packaging company.
2. **Taiwan geopolitical risk**: All major facilities in Taiwan.
3. **Competition from TSMC packaging**: TSMC's own packaging (CoWoS) competes for high-end AI chip packaging.
4. **CPO still early**: Full CPO adoption at scale is 2027+ timeline.

### Verdict: HOLD
ASE is the packaging infrastructure layer for photonic IC deployment -- the company that physically packages SiPh chips, optical engines, and CPO modules for the AI data center. The dedicated SiPh platform, Lightmatter partnership, and CPO demonstrations are real and validated. At $25-30B market cap, it's a large-cap way to play the packaging bottleneck. Would upgrade to BUY when SiPh packaging revenue exceeds $500M annually or CPO enters volume production.
