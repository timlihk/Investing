# KLIC - Kulicke & Soffa Industries
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 62/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Kulicke & Soffa has pivoted from traditional wire bonding into advanced AI packaging with TCB (thermocompression bonding) orders for silicon photonics CPO applications and TSMC CoWoS qualification. The company received SiPh-specific CPO bonding orders and its vertical wire technology targets AI memory packaging. However, K&S is the #2 behind Besi in advanced packaging and faces a more challenging competitive position, making Besi the preferred play in this layer.
