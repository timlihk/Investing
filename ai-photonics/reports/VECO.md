# VECO - Veeco Instruments
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 63/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Veeco is the leading MOCVD (metal organic chemical vapor deposition) equipment maker for compound semiconductor epitaxy -- the critical process that grows the InP and GaAs crystal layers that become every laser chip inside AI optical transceivers. Every Lumentum EML, every Coherent VCSEL, and every InP device starts with epitaxial growth on a Veeco (or Aixtron) MOCVD reactor. Merging with Axcelis Technologies ($4.4B combined) to create a broader semiconductor equipment company, Veeco also partnered with imec on barium titanate integration for next-gen silicon photonics modulators.

### Key Metrics
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Products | MOCVD, laser annealing, ion beam etch, MBE |
| Key Application | InP/GaAs/GaN epitaxial growth for photonic chips |
| Lumina+ MOCVD | New system for high-volume photonics production |

| MOCVD Customers | Every InP/GaAs laser chip maker globally |

### Moat: Moderate-Strong (3/5)
- MOCVD is a duopoly: Veeco and Aixtron (German, also publicly traded: AIXA.DE) control the market.
- Every compound semiconductor laser chip needs MOCVD epitaxial growth -- there is no alternative process.
- Lumina+ system specifically targets high-volume photonics production.
- imec partnership on BaTiO3/SiPh integration positions for next-gen modulators.

### Supply Chain Position
**Upstream equipment for epitaxial growth**:
```
Veeco MOCVD → grows InP/GaAs epitaxial layers → on AXT/JX substrates → becomes laser chips
                                                                      → at Lumentum/Coherent/Sivers
```
- **Competes with**: Aixtron (AIXA.DE, German-listed MOCVD duopoly partner)
- **Sells to**: Every InP/GaAs chip maker, including Lumentum, Coherent, WIN Semi, IQE

### Verdict: HOLD
Veeco is the MOCVD equipment chokepoint for compound semiconductor photonic chips -- a critical upstream tool that every laser maker needs. The Axcelis merger creates a larger combined entity but adds complexity. Aixtron (AIXA.DE) is the European duopoly partner worth monitoring. For photonics equipment exposure, Veeco/Axcelis or Aixtron provide direct upstream tool access.

**Final Score: 63/100**
