# AIXA.DE - Aixtron SE
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 65/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Aixtron is the dominant half of the MOCVD equipment duopoly (with Veeco), commanding 70-90% market share in compound semiconductor deposition tools used to grow every InP laser, GaAs VCSEL, and GaN power device in the AI data center stack. Described as "arguably the most underappreciated AI beneficiary in the market," Aixtron's optoelectronics tools directly enable the 800G/1.6T optical transceiver supply chain. However, 2026 revenue is guided flat to slightly down (~€520M) due to SiC overcapacity, with growth concentrated in optoelectronics and GaN power for AI data centers.

### Moat Analysis
**Rating: Very Strong (4/5)**

- **70-90% MOCVD market share**: Near-monopoly in compound semiconductor epitaxy equipment (sharing with Veeco, which is more GaN-focused).
- **Every InP/GaAs device**: All EML lasers, DFB lasers, VCSELs, and compound semi photonic devices need MOCVD growth. Aixtron tools are the manufacturing prerequisite.
- **SMART Photonics**: European InP foundry accelerating production setup with Aixtron G10-AsP system.
- **Dual AI exposure**: Optoelectronics (laser chips for transceivers) AND GaN power (for AI data center power delivery).

### Supply Chain Position
**Duopoly with Veeco in MOCVD equipment**:
```
Aixtron MOCVD → grows InP/GaAs/GaN epitaxial layers → on substrates (AXT/JX) → becomes laser/power chips
```
- Sells to: Lumentum, Coherent, WIN Semi, IQE, Sivers, SMART Photonics, and all compound semi chip makers
- Strongest growth: optoelectronics for AI optical communications

### Verdict: HOLD
Aixtron is the European MOCVD leader with the highest market share in the industry, directly enabling every compound semiconductor laser chip for AI optical transceivers. The "most underappreciated AI beneficiary" label is earned -- without Aixtron tools, no InP lasers get made. However, the flat 2026 guidance (SiC overcapacity offset) limits near-term re-rating. Would upgrade to BUY if optoelectronics segment growth accelerates to 30%+ and SiC headwinds fade.
