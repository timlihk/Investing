# JEN.DE - Jenoptik AG
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 50/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Jenoptik is a German photonics company with ~€1.05B revenue, 60% from photonic solutions serving semiconductor equipment and industrial optical systems. While positioned to benefit from AI data center investments eventually (semiconductor equipment cycles), revenue declined 6% in FY2025 due to weak semiconductor equipment and automotive demand. Too cyclical and not directly AI photonics-focused for our thesis, but a European photonics infrastructure play for patient investors.
