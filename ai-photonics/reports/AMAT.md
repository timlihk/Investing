# AMAT - Applied Materials (Context Note)
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 65/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Applied Materials is the world's largest semiconductor equipment company with a strategic collaboration with GlobalFoundries to build a waveguide fabrication facility in Singapore for AI photonics. While AMAT at $170B+ market cap is primarily a logic/memory deposition equipment company, its photonics initiative signals that the SiPh supply chain is becoming important enough for the #1 equipment maker to invest directly. AMAT's deposition tools are also essential for growing the thin-film layers in every SiPh chip at Tower, GF, UMC, and STM.

### Supply Chain Position
AMAT sells deposition and etch equipment TO every SiPh foundry:
- Tower Semiconductor, GlobalFoundries, UMC, X-FAB, STMicroelectronics all use AMAT tools
- The GF Singapore photonics fab partnership is a direct AMAT photonics investment

### Verdict: HOLD
AMAT is essential upstream equipment for all SiPh manufacturing, but photonics is a tiny fraction of the $170B+ business. The GF Singapore collaboration signals growing importance. For pure photonics equipment exposure, Aixtron (MOCVD) and Disco (dicing) are more targeted.
