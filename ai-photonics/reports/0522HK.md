# 0522.HK - ASMPT Limited
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 66/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
ASMPT is the world's leading semiconductor assembly and packaging equipment company, uniquely positioned at the photonics assembly layer through its AMICRA subsidiary -- the only equipment maker offering sub-micron (±0.3μm) die bonding for silicon photonics chip packaging. Every SiPh transceiver chip needs ASMPT/AMICRA equipment to attach laser dies to photonic ICs. With HK$14.5B revenue (+10%), bookings up 17.5%, and net profit surging 164% in 2025 on AI-driven demand, ASMPT is the equipment chokepoint of photonic assembly.

### Moat Analysis
**Rating: Strong (3.5/5)**

- **AMICRA photonic die bonding monopoly**: Only equipment maker offering ±0.3μm accuracy for SiPh chip-on-submount (CoS) assembly. Used by every SiPh transceiver maker globally.
- **Advanced packaging leadership**: TCB (thermo-compression bonding) record revenue for AI chip packaging. Hybrid bonding capability for next-gen AI chiplets.
- **Broad equipment portfolio**: Die bonding + wire bonding + TCB + hybrid bonding + SMT. Full assembly equipment stack.
- **Dilution risk**: AMICRA/photonics is one division of a diversified equipment company. Overall business includes slower-growing SMT and traditional packaging segments.

### Supply Chain Position
ASMPT/AMICRA sells die bonding equipment TO:
- **Tower Semiconductor (TSEM)**: SiPh chip packaging
- **Fabrinet (FN)**: Transceiver assembly
- **Innolight (300308.SZ)**: Module packaging
- **Coherent (COHR), Lumentum (LITE)**: Optical component assembly

### Catalysts
1. **SiPh volume production ramp**: Every new Tower Semi/GF SiPh wafer needs AMICRA die bonding equipment to become a packaged chip.
2. **CPO transition**: Co-packaged optics requires even more precise die bonding -- AMICRA's sub-micron accuracy is essential.
3. **AI advanced packaging growth**: TCB and hybrid bonding for AI chiplets are high-growth, high-margin markets.
4. **Bookings accelerating**: +17.5% in FY25, Q1 2026 guided +29.5% YoY.

### Risks
- Photonics is one division of a diversified company -- limits re-rating potential
- HK listing with limited coverage outside Asia
- Equipment business is cyclical and lumpy
- Competition from Besi, Kulicke & Soffa in die bonding (though not at sub-micron SiPh precision)

### Verdict: HOLD
ASMPT owns the photonic assembly equipment chokepoint through AMICRA but the overall business is too diversified for a pure photonics play. At ~$4.5-5B market cap, the company is reasonably valued and the profit surge (+164%) signals operating leverage from AI demand. Best for HK-focused investors seeking equipment exposure to SiPh packaging. For US investors, Fabrinet (FN) offers more direct photonic assembly exposure.
