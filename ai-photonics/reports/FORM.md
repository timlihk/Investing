# FORM - FormFactor Inc
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 67/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
FormFactor is the leading probe card maker for semiconductor wafer testing, and has strategically expanded into silicon photonics testing through its acquisition of Keystone Photonics (Dec 2025). Every SiPh wafer that Tower Semiconductor and GlobalFoundries produce must be tested at wafer level using probe cards -- FormFactor is building the test infrastructure for the photonics era. With $215M Q4 2025 revenue, a path to $850M target model, and SiPh market growing 30% annually, FormFactor is an under-the-radar equipment play on photonic chip volume.

### Moat Analysis
**Rating: Moderate-Strong (3.5/5)**
- Leading probe card maker with Keystone Photonics adding SiPh optical probing capability.
- First fully automated edge-coupling system for SiPh wafer test -- addresses a critical bottleneck in photonic IC production.
- Every SiPh chip needs wafer-level testing before dicing. FormFactor + Keystone creates an end-to-end SiPh test platform.
- Competition from Technoprobe, Japan Electronic Materials, MPI.
