# 5803.T - Fujikura Ltd
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 68/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Fujikura is Japan's hottest AI infrastructure stock -- surging 160% in 2025 as AI data center demand drove optical fiber orders, and now tripling US optical fiber output for AI infrastructure. With ¥6.71T ($45B) market cap, 16% EBITDA margins, and unanimous analyst Buy ratings, Fujikura has emerged as the Japanese Corning equivalent. However, the stock is near its ATH and the AI fiber thesis is now well-priced.

### Verdict: HOLD
Fujikura is a quality Japan fiber play riding the AI infrastructure wave, but the 160% surge and near-ATH price mean most of the re-rating has happened. At 36.8x P/E, the valuation assumes continued strong fiber demand. Better as a Japan portfolio holding than a fresh entry. Corning (GLW) offers more direct US hyperscaler exposure for fiber-focused investors.
