# XFAB.PA - X-FAB Silicon Foundries SE
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 56/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
X-FAB is a European specialty foundry (Euronext Paris-listed) building a silicon photonics platform with InP-on-Silicon heterogeneous integration capability -- a collaboration with SMART Photonics to achieve >120 GHz modulator bandwidth vs 70 GHz ceiling for pure SiPh. Combined with LIGENTEC's SiN photonics integration, X-FAB is creating a multi-material photonics foundry ecosystem. Still early (production ramp-up 2027), but represents Europe's foundry bet on heterogeneous photonic integration.

### Moat: Emerging (2/5)
- InP-on-Silicon heterogeneous integration is cutting-edge but unproven at volume.
- European foundry alternative to Tower Semi/GlobalFoundries for SiPh.
- Multi-material platform (SOI + InP + SiN + LiNbO3) is differentiated.
- But SiPh is a small fraction of total foundry revenue; Tower has 85% share.

### Verdict: HOLD
X-FAB is building an interesting heterogeneous photonics foundry platform, but it's very early (production 2027) and SiPh is a small segment of the broader specialty foundry. Tower Semi and GlobalFoundries are far ahead in volume SiPh. Monitor for InP-on-Silicon production qualification and first customer ramp. Niche European play for investors who believe heterogeneous integration will outperform pure silicon photonics.
