# TER - Teradyne Inc
## Date: 2026-03-24
## Score: 72/100

### Thesis (2-3 sentences)
Teradyne is the #2 ATE (automatic test equipment) company that strategically acquired Quantifi Photonics -- a PIC (photonic integrated circuit) testing leader -- to enter the photonic IC test market directly. With $3.19B FY2025 revenue (+13%), semiconductor test growing 45-90% YoY on AI demand, and AI now driving 70% of Q1 2026 revenue, Teradyne is the Advantest duopoly partner with a dedicated photonic IC testing acquisition. The Quantifi Photonics deal makes Teradyne the first major ATE company to offer scalable PIC test solutions.

### Moat Analysis
**Rating: Strong (4/5)**

- **ATE duopoly with Advantest**: Teradyne and Advantest control the semiconductor test market. Teradyne is stronger in compute/SoC testing.
- **Quantifi Photonics acquisition**: First major ATE company with dedicated photonic IC test capability. As SiPh/CPO volumes grow, PIC testing becomes a critical bottleneck.
- **AI driving 70% of revenue**: Direct exposure to every AI chip being manufactured.
- **Compute segment +90%**: GPU/AI ASIC testing is the fastest-growing segment.

### Supply Chain Position
- **Sells to**: Every semiconductor manufacturer (TSMC, Samsung, Intel, Tower Semi, GF)
- **PIC testing**: Quantifi Photonics enables testing of photonic ICs from Tower Semi, GlobalFoundries
- **Competes with**: Advantest (6857.T) in ATE, FormFactor/Aehr in photonic wafer test
- **Robotic automation**: Also owns Universal Robots (industrial robotics division)

### Catalysts
1. **Quantifi Photonics integration**: Scalable PIC test solutions for the SiPh/CPO market.
2. **AI 70% of revenue**: Direct correlation to AI chip production volumes.
3. **Compute +90% growth**: GPU testing demand accelerating.
4. **Q1 2026 record guidance**: $1.15-1.25B shows continued momentum.

### Risks
1. **Well-known large-cap**: Not under-discovered at $25-30B market cap.
2. **Cyclical ATE spending**: Semi test capex can swing significantly.
3. **Quantifi early stage**: PIC testing acquisition is new -- revenue contribution will take time.
4. **Robotics dilution**: Universal Robots segment diversifies away from pure semiconductor focus.
